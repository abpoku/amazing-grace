About Us

Vision Statement. At Amazing Grace Kiddie Kare, our vision is to nurture a generation of confident, compassionate, and curious learners who grow in wisdom, creativity, and character. We envision a safe, joyful, and enriching environment where every child is valued, inspired, and prepared to thrive in life.

Mission Statement. Our mission is to provide high-quality, faith-rooted early childhood education that supports the whole child—spiritually, emotionally, socially, and academically. Through caring relationships, purposeful play, and a strong foundation in early learning, we partner with families to build strong beginnings for bright futures.

Amazing Grace — your child's future truly begins here.
